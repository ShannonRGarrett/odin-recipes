Grilled PB & J

( PB&J just got WAYY better )

Description:

Full transparency here- I have never been a fan of PB&Js. I will not just side and make myself that sandwhich; I prefer PB&Banana or PB&Honey. All that changed when I was introduced to the Grilled PB&J!
This sandwhich is like no other. So melty and warm! I eat it as a dessert!

Ingredients:

  • White bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (whatever flavor you fancy)
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Make your PB&J as you normal would
    1. Lay out your two slices of bread
    2. Place a good amount of Peanut Butter on one piece
    3. Jelly is placed on the other side
  2. On a griddle, place butter in the shape of your bread (want enough for your whole piece of bread)
  3. Once the butter is place, put your made PB&J on the butter
  4. Let that grill that side of the sandwhich, then repeat the butter steps for the other side
