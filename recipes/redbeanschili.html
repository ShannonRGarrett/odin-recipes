Red Beans Chili

( A mix between Red Beans and Rice and Chili )

Description:

This recipe was founded the same way all great things are found... by chnace. It all started when my husband made the mistake of asking me to cook; you see I am not the chef in the family. Well I did what he asked me to do, which was chop up the sausage and the 'Holy Trinity' for the Red Beans and Rice that we were going to have. After that was done he told he it is like a 4 hour process and he had meetings that night so I would have to baby sit the meal. NOOO Thanks! Thats when he was like okay what else can we do??!

And so Red Beans Chili was born.

Ingredients:

  • Red Beans
  • Sausage
  • 'Holy Trinity'
  • Ground beef
  • Chili pack
  • Pace salsa

Steps:

  1. Soak the Red Beans overnight or place them in the instapot for an hour
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Chop all the ingredients
  4. Once the beans are ready put all the ingredients into the Instapot and stir
  5. Add extra heat, if wanted
  6. Put Instapot on Chili setting and let it do all the work!
